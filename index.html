A Slice of Pairault

Welcome to my website! The purpose of this site is to provide you with a little bit of information about myself. The creation of this website is for the purposes of completing an assignment, and the duration of this website is pending the completion of my university quarter.

This website was made possible by utilizing the template Phantom, available on HTML5 UP, and I made personal edits to the HTML to make the website more my own. HTML5UP provide many free, fully responsive, frameworks in HTML. This makes it incredibly easier to make custom websites, and to then be able to host them through GitHub. Unsplash was also used for all head images on each page.

This website has three pages to tell you a little bit more about myself, and what I do. Naturally the About Me page will tell you a little bit more about myself. The Portfolio page is meant to provide you with my skillset, working field, and the trajectory I would like to go in. And finally, the Gallery page is meant to provide you with some of my creative skillset, while minimal, in various mediums.
